OVERVIEW

Onmyoji RPG

Onmyoji is a turn-based fantasy strategy game with PVP or PVE battles, where you can strengthen your beloved shikigami to build your dream tactical team and defeat various demons to become the ultimate onmyoji. Cross-platform play available for both Steam and English mobile versions.

Game genre :RPG-turn-based

Release date:19/06/2018

Publisher   :Netease

SHIKIGAMI

Shikigami

Shikigami is summoned by the onmyouji to help them. The summoned Shikigami will be on the contracted list. When enough shikigami pieces are collected, they can summon that shikigami. After summoning, the shikigami will be available in the Shikigami section. During battle, shikigami are summoned to fight using paper dolls.

Rarity

Shikigami has SSR, SR, R, and N rarity. Rarity affects the number of pieces to summon a shikigami.

Shiranui – A Ly

Rare: SSR

Receive: Summon, Demon Parade

SSR Onmyouji shikigami has 2 sets of skills, normally a basic one-time attack support for the whole team. If you activate the transformation, the skill set will completely change into main damage with powerful and wide area attack skills to turn the game upside down when all teammates die.

Kidou Maru

Rare: SSR

Receive: Summon, Demon Parade

Kidou Maru is an SSR-rated Onmyouji Shikigami with the role of dealing damage in the squad.

Kinnara

Rare: SSR

Receive: Summon, Demon Parade

Kinnara is a female shikigami of Onmyoji, classified as SSR. This mythical creature is rooted in Indian mythology, appearing in both Brahminism, Hinduism, and Buddhism. Described in many forms, half human half horse or half human half bird, but the most popular is still half human half bird. When in male form they are called Kinnara, in goddess form they will call Kinnari.
